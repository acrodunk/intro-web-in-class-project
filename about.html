ORIGINS

Dunk Ninja began as Planet Dunk and was first presented to competitors in 2015. As the first competitive dunk course, Planet Dunk now known as Dunk Ninja is the beginning of dunking as fitness. The 2017 edition includes 7 trampolines, a 10ft Tower of Power and 1 challenging mission - to complete all dunks in the fastest time.

RULES

The rules are simple. Complete a different dunk off each trampoline in the fastest time. Dunks will be valued according to the International Freestyle Dunk Federation's standard. Duplicated dunks will be penalized 30 seconds. Throw-in dunks will be penalized 30 seconds. Time ends when the last dunk is completed.

STRATEGIES

A good rule of thumb is your dunk path starting at one end and going immediately back to the other end of the course. For instance, if your first dunk is on the East Coast trampolines your next dunk should be on one of the West Coast trampolines and back and forth until completion.
