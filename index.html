DUNK NINJA

Competitive Freestyle Dunk Course

DUNK NINJA is a freestyle dunk course designed for solo, 2-man and team dunk contests. The Dunk Ninja freestyle dunk course is designed to showcase the repertoire, stamina and athleticism of freestyle dunk athletes while challenging them to optimize their dunk repertoire and strategize their FLY-ness.
